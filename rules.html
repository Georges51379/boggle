Boggle is a word searching game that best fits people and kids from 8 years old till 100 years old.

  • Rules:
  • Words must be at least three letters in length.
  • Each letter must be a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal neighbor of the one before it.
  • No individual letter square may be used more than once in a word.
  • No capitalized words or hyphenated words are allowed.
  • You have 4 minutes 10 seconds to insert as much words as you can.
  • Words with red underline, are considered wrong.
  • At the end, your total score will be displayed.
  • When pressing shuffle, the game will start or restart.

  • Score:
  • You will not receive any point for words less than 3 characters.
  • You will receive +1 for words that are 3 characters long.
  • You will receive +2 for words that are 4 characters long.
  • You will receive +3 for words that are 5 characters long.
  • You will receive +4 for words that are 6 characters long.
  • You will receive +5 for words that are 7 characters long.
  • You will receive +6 for words that are 8 characters long.
